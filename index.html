Principles

Altair Industries’s Philosophy

  • Strive for Excellence – On execution, everything that is executed from Altair Industries must be at highest dedication to quality.  We take pride in everything that has our name behind it.  Excellence does not naturally come out in business, gifted minds take countless of trials and errors, endure prolonged period of external and internal doubts, and go through major difficulties in attaining to their excellency.  We want to be the best of our capability in business, thus, we value dedication, continuously improving, open-minded, and, most of all, hard-work.
  • Deal Honorably – We have a very long term view on doing business.  Long term means life-time.  We want to do business with our suppliers, customers, and other stakeholders on life-time basis.  And if we are lucky enough to survive ups and downs of the market and have a second, third, or later generations that run our company – they will also do business on life-time view basis.  Thus toward each and every transaction, it is either a happy transaction or we will continue to work it out toward happiness and for the relationship in the long-run.

Our Mission

  • Stable and Reliable Supply Chain – Altair Industries works to build a supply chain of hardwood from the US and EU to import to Vietnam for furniture making. We aim for a cost effective, operation efficiency, and supply stableness to enhance the overall supply chain of our business alliance.
